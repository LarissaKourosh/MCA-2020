Task 2

Transcription

Below I transcriped my composition piece from Week2 - Lonely-Justin Bieber. I have presented parts of both the original notated version compared to that of the transcriped Musescore version of which I have discussed below.

Comparison of original composition to that of the transcribed version

When looking at both the original composition to that of the transribed version of the piece, it is clear that the original composition is more successful in effectively conveying the tune and theme of the original song. The transcribed version somewhat jumbles the notes together and makes it less clear what the tune/melody is. Although the transcribed version of the song resembles that of the original composition, it doesn't reflect the melody in any way but instead makes it sound like the notes are combined together.
