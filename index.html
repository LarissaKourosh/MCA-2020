Music curation and Analytics 2020

Week 1: Music Dataset

Task 1

Dataset theme

The theme of my dataset is Canadian Singer and Songwriter Justin Bieber. Having made his debut in 2009 with his album My World, Justin Bieber has since sold an estimated 150 million records worldwide, making him one of the worlds best selling music artists. His 2009 Album 'My World' went platinum in several countries, and again in 2015 he had his first number one song 'What do you mean?', 2017 he broke the all time record of most consecutive weeks in Top 100 with 'Despacito', and then again in 2020 his album 'Changes' made him the youngest artist to have several albums reach the top spot on Billboard Top 200. Having followed Justin Biebers journey from the beginning and being a fan of his music, i was encouraged to pick him for my dataset theme as my knowledge on his music will help me throughout the tasks.


Task 2

Data Types availability and its current manifestation

Descriptive data

On Justin Biebers official website users have access to a wide variety of information about all his released music. This information consists of tracks within albums, favourites and platforms the music can be accessed from. Spotify, Apple Music, Youtube and Deezer are just examples of the main streaming services his music is available from. Amongst these platforms, music is organised by albums, singles and 'essentials playlists', consisting of his collectively most played songs. Spotify have a playlist called 'This is Justin Bieber' that is an example of an essentials playlist.

Notated Data

In regards to Notated Data, Sheet music for Justin Biebers songs can be accessed from various online sheet music catalogues; musicnotes.com is a notable example of where his sheet music can be accessed at a small cost.

Acoustic Data

In terms of acoustic data, Spotify, Youtube and Apple Music are examples of main streaming services that Justin Biebers Acoustic Data can be found. Not only published from his official account, but covers, concerts and fan videos can be found from various other channels/accounts.
